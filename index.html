about

Spider-Man is one of the most popular and commercially successful superheroes, created by writer-editor Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko for Marvel Comics. He first appeared in Amazing Fantasy #15 in August 1962 and has since become a global cultural icon featured in comic books, television shows, films, and video games.
