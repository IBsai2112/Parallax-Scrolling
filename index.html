about

Kalyani Priyadarshan (born April 5, 1993) is an acclaimed Indian actress primarily working in Malayalam, Tamil, and Telugu cinema. Daughter of director Priyadarshan and actress Lissy, she debuted in the Telugu film Hello (2017), winning the Filmfare Award for Best Female Debut – South. She is known for her roles in Hridayam, Bro Daddy, and Thallumaala.
